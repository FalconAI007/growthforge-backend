Ad Spend is Separate
The management fee is separate from your actual ad spend. You're charged for:
- Management fee (GrowthForge Media services)
- Actual ad spend (paid to Meta/Facebook directly)
- Landing page hosting (if applicable)

Example:
If you allocate $1,000/month for ads, your costs would be:
- Ad spend: $1,000
- Management fee: $400
- Total monthly investment: $1,400

SUCCESS REQUIREMENTS
====================
For Facebook Ads to work best:

1. Clear Sales Process
- You need a way to follow up with leads quickly
- Can close leads within 24-48 hours
- Have a system to track which leads convert

2. Reasonable Margins
- Customer lifetime value must justify lead cost
- Generally need 10:1 LTV to lead cost ratio
- Break-even point should be under 30 days

3. Local Business Model
- Serve a specific geographic area
- Can clearly define target audience
- Local service/product focus

4. Commitment to Process
- Willing to let us test and optimize for 30-60 days
- Can provide feedback on lead quality
- Willing to track conversion data
- Can maintain ad budget for 90+ days

5. Responsive Communication
- Available for weekly strategy calls
- Can answer questions about audience/business
- Provide feedback on lead quality
- Communicate pain points quickly

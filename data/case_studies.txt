CASE STUDY 1: EMERGENCY PLUMBING SERVICE
==========================================
CLIENT: SafeFlow Plumbing (Local Service Provider)
LOCATION: Denver, Colorado
TIMELINE: 6 months engagement
INDUSTRY: Plumbing & Emergency Services

THE CHALLENGE
-----------
SafeFlow Plumbing was a well-established plumbing company with a strong reputation but they were struggling with inconsistent lead flow. They relied primarily on:
- Yellow Pages listings (outdated)
- Word-of-mouth referrals (unreliable)
- Google local listings (minimal activity)

Their Problem:
- 5-8 service calls per week (inconsistent)
- Long gaps between jobs
- Mostly reactive customers (emergency calls)
- No proactive lead generation system
- Couldn't scale without more visibility

Monthly Revenue Goal: $8,000-12,000 in service jobs
Current Lead Source: 90% word-of-mouth, 10% Yellow Pages

THE STRATEGY
-----------
After analyzing their market, we developed a Facebook Ads strategy targeting:
- Homeowners aged 35-65 in Denver metro area
- Interests: Home improvement, property maintenance, DIY repairs
- Behaviors: Home related interests and online shoppers
- Geographic: 10 mile radius from their service area

Campaign Angles Tested:
1. "Emergency plumbing? We're 30 minutes away" (response time angle)
2. "Hidden water leaks? Here's how to tell" (educational angle)
3. "$75 off first service + free inspection" (offer angle)
4. Before/after photos of pipes, repairs (social proof angle)

Ad Creative:
- Carousel ads showing common plumbing problems
- Video testimonials from past customers
- Educational posts about pipe maintenance
- Seasonal promotions (winterization, spring repairs)

MONTH BY MONTH RESULTS
----------------------
Month 1 (Setup & Testing):
- Ad Spend: $1,000
- Management Fee: $400
- Leads Generated: 32
- Cost Per Lead: $31.25
- Status: Testing phase, collecting data

Month 2 (Optimization):
- Ad Spend: $1,000
- Management Fee: $400
- Leads Generated: 48
- Cost Per Lead: $20.83
- Optimization: Paused underperforming ad variations, increased budget to winning ads

Month 3 (Scaling):
- Ad Spend: $1,200
- Management Fee: $400
- Leads Generated: 72
- Cost Per Lead: $16.67
- Optimization: Expanded winning audiences, tested new geographic zones

Month 4-6 (Optimization & Growth):
- Ad Spend: $1,200-1,500
- Management Fee: $400-600
- Monthly Leads: 85-100
- Cost Per Lead: $13-15
- Conversion Rate: 22% (leads to actual jobs)

6-MONTH RESULTS
---------------
Total Leads Generated: 412
Average Cost Per Lead: $17.43
Estimated Jobs Booked: 90 (22% conversion rate)
Average Job Revenue: $1,800
Estimated Revenue: $162,000

Client Outcome:

CASE STUDY 3: DIGITAL MARKETING AGENCY (B2B)
==============================================
CLIENT: TechGrow Marketing
LOCATION: Phoenix, Arizona
TIMELINE: 5 months engagement
INDUSTRY: Professional Services (B2B Marketing)

THE CHALLENGE
-----------
TechGrow is a digital marketing agency targeting small to mid-size businesses (SMBs). They were:
- Struggling to book strategy calls with decision makers
- Competing against larger agencies
- Long sales cycle (30-60 days)
- High customer lifetime value ($15,000-50,000+)

Goals:
- Generate 15-20 qualified leads per month
- Target business owners and marketing managers
- Focus on SMBs with $500k-$5M annual revenue
- Increase brand awareness in their market

THE STRATEGY
-----------
Key Decision: Use a lead generation funnel instead of direct lead capture

Funnel Architecture:
Step 1: Lead Magnet - "The SMB Marketing Checklist" (free download)
Step 2: Email Sequence - 5-part nurture sequence over 10 days
Step 3: Webinar Offer - "How to 2x Your Lead Generation in 90 Days"
Step 4: Strategy Call Booking - Book a free 30-minute strategy audit

Campaign Strategy:
- Phase 1: Drive traffic to lead magnet (week 1-2)
- Phase 2: Nurture leads via email (week 3+)
- Phase 3: Qualify and book strategy calls (ongoing)

Audience Targeting:
- Business owners and marketing managers
- Age: 35-65
- Interests: Marketing, business growth, lead generation
- Behaviors: Business decision makers, active online
- Exclude: Competitors, agencies

Ad Creative:
- Educational carousel posts about marketing strategies
- Video testimonials from successful clients
- Case study content (shorter versions)
- Founder advice posts (thought leadership)
